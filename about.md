## Lingxiang Hu (胡凌翔)

I am a researcher at **Tencent**, based in Beijing, China. I obtained both my B.S. and M.S. degrees from **Northeastern University (东北大学)** in Shenyang.

### Research Interests

My research spans across several areas:

- **AI & LLM**: Large Language Model agents, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), LLM benchmarking
- **Advertising Technology**: AI-powered advertising analytics
- **Network Systems**: SmartNIC-based packet scheduling, ML-driven traffic classification

### Professional Experience
