📖 About Me

I am a researcher at Tencent, Beijing, China. I obtained both my B.S. and M.S. degrees from Northeastern University (东北大学), Shenyang.

My research interests include Large Language Models (LLMs), AI Agents, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), SmartNIC / Network Systems, and Machine Learning for Networking.
